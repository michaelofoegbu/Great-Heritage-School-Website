HISTORY

The School commenced with Creche in February 1995, The Nursery and Primary classes started in September 1997, The school continued to grow from strength to strength as each year approaches, The primary section started in year 200 with ten pupils in different classes' The school employed more staffs as the pupils increases, The staffs were trained to work towards the actualization of the vision of the school.

MISSION

  • OUR MISSION IS TO PROVIDE QUALITY EDUCATION FOR ALL CHILDREN REGISTERED IN THE SCHOOL WITH QUALITY AND BALANCED EDUCATION, UNDER SUPERVISION OF DEDICATED AND QUALIFIED STAFFS.

  • GREAT HERITAGE IS COMMITED TO THE PURSUIT OF AN INTERNATIONALLY ACCEPTED STANDARD OF ACADEMIC EXCELLENCE.

VISION

  • TO PROVIDE A BALANCED EDUCATION THAT WILL ENHANCE THE FULL DEVELOPMENT OF EACH CHILD'S CHARACTER-SPIRITUALLY, EMOTIONALLY, INELLECTALLY, PHYSICALLY AND SOCIALLY.

  • TO TRAIN PUPILS WITH THE NECESSARY EDUCATIONAL INFRASTRUCTURE AND FACILITIES THAT WILL SERVE AS A SPRING BOARD OUR THE POTENTIALS IN EACH CHILD.
